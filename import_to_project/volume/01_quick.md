# Quick

### Description

Upload volumes or labeled data from you computer with auto-detection of annotation format.

### Overview

Drag and drop files, folders or an archive. Supervisely will automatically detect annotation format, verify, and import your data.
When uploading to an existing project, Supervisely will automatically validate and merge classes, tags, and annotations.

**Supported video formats:** `.nrrd` `.dcm`

**Annotation formats (click to see instructions):**

- Volumes without annotations
- [Supervisely]()

<br>

**Single volume file size limits:**

- Community Free plan: 150MB
- Community Pro plan: 150MB
- Enterprise Edition: Unlimited
